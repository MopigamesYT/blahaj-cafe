The staff team

Here is each role on our staff team, and how they are here to help you!

~The Founder~

Creator of the server, report instances of admin abuse or server recommendations to her

~The Administrators~

High level staff who oversee all other mods and have high level permissions on the server, escalate matters to them if necessary

~The Moderators~

People who are usually responsible for banning people who joined that are trolls, mediating chats, etc., usually you want to address any concerns with them first

~The Jr. Moderators~

Entry level staff who sift through chat to make sure people follow rules, they do not have permissions to ban people so contact higher level staff for ban concerns
